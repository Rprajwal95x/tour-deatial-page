Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg Package Highlights:

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive in Srinagar and transfer to your luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake. Enjoy a traditional Kashmiri welcome and explore the serene waters on a Shikara ride. Spend the evening admiring the stunning sunset over the mountains.

Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit the Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, known for their exquisite landscaping. Explore the vibrant local markets for handicrafts and traditional Pashmina shawls before returning to your houseboat for dinner.

Day 3: Day Trip to Gulmarg

Head to Gulmarg, renowned for its meadows and skiing resorts. Enjoy a Gondola ride to Khilanmarg, where you can take in spectacular views of the snow-capped mountains. Spend the day indulging in outdoor activities such as skiing or trekking.
